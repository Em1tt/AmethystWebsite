This article will teach you the following:

  • Where the shops are located at
  • What we use as money

Where can I find shops?

Shops are usually located near the spawning locations.
- In Season 3 we have not yet established a Shopping district.

What is our currency

Our main currency is diamonds. Although, currency may change depending on the shop. As a shop owner you're free to decide what currency you want there to be used.
